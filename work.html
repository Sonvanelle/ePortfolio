Drawings and code. There are many like it, but these are mine.

I love art, both the art of coding and drawing. Although I may not be the best, and I have a ways to go, so there's no stopping now!

I'm using Python as a springboard to learn programming concepts and other languages. Next up will be Java and the Android bit of Java!

I also enjoy design and drawing. Most of my PowerPoint themes are created on the spot, and I aim for a different design language for each one, though I tend to sway to the minimalist side of things.

I'm trying to find a style I like by doodling in GIMP ever so often, but drawing with a mouse sure is rough.
